From data to diary in four steps.

Nothing complex. Nothing cloud. Just you, your data, and your story.

01

Export Health data

Health app → your profile → Export All Health Data. Takes about 30 seconds.

02

Drop the ZIP

Drag your exported ZIP into PulseJournal. It reads locally — no upload, no server.

03

Choose your tone

Pick a narrative style and hit Generate. Ollama does the rest, entirely on your machine.

04

Read your story

Your diary is ready. Nothing left your Mac. Export as Markdown or enjoy it right here.
